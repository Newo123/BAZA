The modern world of dance consists of many styles, but only Jazz Funk will give you an idea of the real drive, sexuality, confidence, energy and incendiary dance. This is one of the most popular trends, because jazz-funk attracts views, whether you want it or not. The combination of different styles - hip-hop, jazz, waxing and others - allows a dancer to stand out on any dance floor, feel confident and amaze others. Jazz funk has absorbed the best movements of all directions, while seasoning the dance with audacity, sexuality, plasticity and crazy drive. Small but clear movements allow you to experiment and improvise: the dance will never look the same.
