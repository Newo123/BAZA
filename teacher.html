Кратко о педагоге

My dance-sports path began at an early age. I went through 4 years of rhythmic gymnastics, as well as 8 years in the choreographic ensemble "Pērlīte", where I received the basics in the dances of different peoples of the world, pop and modern dance. But I continued to look for myself in different dance styles and sports. took various stretching and fitness courses, including getting a “C” category trainer and in 2019 I started teaching stretching in fitness clubs. In 2015, I got my first Breaking class at Baza Dance Company and fell in love. Breaking combines my two passions - dance and sports! I got so carried away that already in 2017 I won prizes in Latvia.
